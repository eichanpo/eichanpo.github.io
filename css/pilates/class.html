Group Class

Group Reformer classes are a wonderful option for clients with prior experience on the Reformer or for those familiar with the basic Pilates repertoire. The Pilates Reformer, invented by Joseph Pilates, is the most well-known piece of Pilates equipment.
Group Reformer classes are limited to 6 to 8 participants per class and require signing up in advance. A minimum of four (4) Private sessions are recommended prior to joining a Group Reformer class for the first time. Alternatively, we would suggest joining a Group Reformer Fundamentals class for safety and apparatus familiarisation purposes.

Private Class

Pilates is highly beneficial to practitioners with a specific goal in mind, be it in relation to general fitness, rehabilitation or recovery, and for those wishing to gain a greater understanding of their bodies’ respective tendencies. The undivided, one-on-one attention derived from our qualified instructors will help you thrive quickly.
And effectively as part of your general fitness routine, especially when recovering from an injury or shoulder and back pain brought about by a sedentary, technology-focused lifestyle. Whether your preference is a moderate or a more advanced session, we are here to ensure that your time on the Pilates apparatuses is spent as constructively as possible.
